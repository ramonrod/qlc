Other comments: Ngbugu has a vigesimal system with five-based substructure.

The ' tɜ̀ ' at beginning (20 - 59 ) is often dropped in rapid speech. Also, ' t͡ʃèpànɜ̀ ',

which means 'more' is intentionally dropped from 26 to 29.

Tone marksː  [v́] = high tone, [v̄] = mid tone and [ v̀ ] = low tone.
